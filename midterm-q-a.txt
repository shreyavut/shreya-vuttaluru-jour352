10.  Why do we use media queries?

We use media queries for responsive web design in order to appropriately format our content for multiple screen sizes, like when you minimize your window or switch from a laptop to a phone.


11. What is the main difference between a serif and a sans-serif font?

Serif fonts have little "ticks" or "strokes" at the end of each letter. Sans-serif fonts do not. Atom uses a sans-serif font.
